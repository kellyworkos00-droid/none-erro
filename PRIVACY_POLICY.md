# Privacy Policy for Kelly OS ERP

**Last Updated:** February 28, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Kelly OS ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Business Information
- Company name, address, and contact details
- Business registration information
- Tax identification numbers
- Banking and financial account information

### 2.2 User Account Information
- Name, email address, and phone number
- Job title and department
- Username and encrypted password
- Role and permission settings
- Login history and activity logs

### 2.3 Customer and Supplier Data
- Contact information (names, emails, phone numbers, addresses)
- Payment history and outstanding balances
- Transaction records and invoices
- Communication preferences

### 2.4 Financial Data
- Sales orders and invoices
- Purchase orders and supplier bills
- Payment records and receipts
- Bank statements and reconciliation data
- Financial reports and analytics

### 2.5 Inventory and Product Data
- Product descriptions, SKUs, and pricing
- Stock levels and warehouse locations
- Product movement history
- Supplier and cost information

### 2.6 Employee Information (if HR module is used)
- Personal identification information
- Employment details and contracts
- Payroll information
- Leave records and attendance data
- Performance evaluations

### 2.7 Technical Information
- IP addresses and device identifiers
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Access times and page views
- Error logs and system diagnostics

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### 3.1 Service Delivery
- Provide core ERP functionality (accounting, inventory, sales, purchasing)
- Process transactions and payments
- Generate financial reports and analytics
- Manage user access and permissions

### 3.2 Business Operations
- Send transactional notifications (invoices, payment reminders)
- Communicate service updates and maintenance schedules
- Provide customer support and technical assistance
- Process payroll and manage employee benefits

### 3.3 Security and Compliance
- Authenticate users and prevent unauthorized access
- Detect and prevent fraud or security breaches
- Maintain audit trails for compliance purposes
- Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

### 3.4 System Improvement
- Analyze usage patterns to improve functionality
- Troubleshoot technical issues
- Develop new features and enhancements
- Optimize system performance

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Data Storage
- All data is stored in secure, encrypted databases
- Database backups are performed daily
- Data is stored on servers located in secure facilities
- Backup retention period: 90 days minimum

### 4.2 Security Measures
- **Encryption:** All data transmitted is encrypted using TLS/SSL protocols
- **Access Control:** Role-based access control (RBAC) system
- **Authentication:** Secure password requirements and optional two-factor authentication
- **Monitoring:** Continuous security monitoring and threat detection
- **Audit Logs:** Complete audit trail of all system activities
- **Data Segregation:** Multi-tenant architecture with data isolation

### 4.3 Data Retention
- Active business data: Retained for the duration of your subscription
- Archived data: Retained for 7 years for compliance purposes
- User activity logs: Retained for 2 years
- Deleted account data: Purged within 30 days of account closure

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 We DO NOT:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your data for marketing purposes
- Use your data for purposes unrelated to ERP functionality

### 5.2 We MAY Share Data With:
- **Service Providers:** Cloud hosting, payment processors, email services (under strict confidentiality agreements)
- **Legal Authorities:** When required by law, court order, or legal process
- **Business Transfers:** In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale (with user notification)
- **Authorized Third Parties:** With your explicit consent only

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Access and Control
- **Right to Access:** Request copies of your personal data
- **Right to Rectification:** Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Right to Deletion:** Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
- **Right to Export:** Download your data in portable format (CSV, Excel, PDF)
- **Right to Restrict Processing:** Limit how we use your data

### 6.2 Data Portability
- Export all your business data at any time
- Standard formats: CSV, Excel, JSON, PDF
- Bulk export available through system settings

### 6.3 Consent Withdrawal
- You may withdraw consent for optional data processing
- Essential data processing required for service delivery may continue
- Account deletion will terminate all data processing

## 7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

### 7.1 Types of Cookies
- **Essential Cookies:** Required for authentication and security
- **Functional Cookies:** Remember your preferences and settings
- **Analytics Cookies:** Help us understand system usage (anonymous)
- **Session Cookies:** Temporary, deleted when you close your browser

### 7.2 Your Cookie Choices
- You can control cookies through browser settings
- Disabling essential cookies may affect system functionality
- We do not use third-party advertising cookies

## 8. International Data Transfers

- Data may be processed in countries where our servers are located
- We ensure adequate data protection measures for international transfers
- We comply with applicable data protection regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)

## 9. Children's Privacy
